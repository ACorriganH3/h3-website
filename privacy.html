"We", "us" and "our" mean H3 Energy Limited, company number 17073106, with registered office at 1 Queen Katherine Street, Kendal, England, LA9 7DG. This policy applies to our site, h3.energy, and was last updated in July 2026.

Who we are

H3 Energy Limited is a company registered in England and Wales. We develop modular ammonia-to-hydrogen systems for industrial customers.

When does this policy apply

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide to us through this website, or when you contact us directly by email. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with other organisations. Any future changes to this policy will be posted on this page.

Your information

Unlike many corporate websites, h3.energy has no contact form, no account system, and no marketing sign-up. We only receive personal information if you choose to email us directly, for example at hello@h3.energy. That may include your name, email address, and whatever else you include in your message.

We do not automatically collect information about how you browse this site. We do not use analytics, advertising pixels, or any tool that profiles visitors.

Reasons for storing your data

Where we hold your information, it is for a legitimate business reason only, responding to your enquiry, and, where relevant, progressing a commercial, partnership, or investment conversation you have initiated with us.

Our website

This site may link to third-party websites, including social platforms. This policy only covers h3.energy; if you follow a link elsewhere, that site's own privacy policy applies.

Sharing your data

We do not share your information with third parties, other than professional advisers (for example, our lawyers or accountants) where reasonably necessary, or where we are required to by law.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
