The H3 approach

Skip the pipeline.
Skip the compressor.

Deliver ammonia. Crack it on site, at pressure, with a patent-pending Pd-membrane reactor. No downstream compression. Pure hydrogen, delivered to the customer's point of use.

Why ammonia

One ammonia tanker
replaces twelve
hydrogen tube trailers.

Global bulk ammonia logistics already exist: century-old infrastructure, not a future bet. H3 turns that existing supply chain into hydrogen, at the point of use.

Ready for tomorrow

Green ammonia.
Same system.

As green ammonia becomes commercially available, customers can transition without replacing equipment or redesigning their processes.
